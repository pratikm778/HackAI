|  | Revenue limit: Fixed price contracts including software development and system integration contracts is recognized using a percentage of completion method. Use of the percentage-ofcompletion method requires the Group to determine the actual costs expended to date as a proportion of the estimated total costs to be incurred. Costs expended have been used to measure progress towards completion as there is a direct relationship between input and productivity.
